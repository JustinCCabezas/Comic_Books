Amazing Characters

There are a lot of amazing characters. The only character that catches everyones eyes is Spider-man. Spider-man has been a major success to Marvel Comics. If you think about it, he's basically a teen who accidentally gets his powers from a mutated spider and develops abilities. Then when Uncle Ben dies, he later wants to fallow in his Uncle's footsteps with responsibility. Spider-man recently became an owner of his very own industry. With his new invention, the Web Watch. He's now became the worlds number one hero.
