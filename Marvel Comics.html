Marvel Comics

Marvel Comics are great. There are so many heroes, villains, and anti-heroes that do their own special way of fighting crime on streets and taking on another alien race. One of my favorite Marvel Comics Event was Civil War. Civil War was about the government forcing heroes to sign SHRA (Super Hero Regestration Act) that prevents heroes from doing their own hero work and lets the government force the heroes to do whatever they wish to do. Iron-Man was on board with the registration, including other famous heroes like Captain Marvel, Wasp, She-hulk, and Stature. Captain America never agreed with the act and said that every hero has their rights just like normal civilians do. Other heroes like Daredevil, Falcon, Invisible Girl, Cloak and Dagger agreed with Cap. Then when things started to stir up, the civil war began.
